# Career Advocate Promotion Flow

## One short summary
Alex checks his performance status with the Career Advocate bot, refines a new goal using SMART coaching, and requests a promotion routed to manager Sarah after eligibility validation and content sanitization.

## Personas
- **Alex** - Senior Developer (Employee)
- **Sarah** - Engineering Manager (Approver)
- **Career Advocate** - AI Agent (career coach and HR assistant)

## Story flow
1. Alex opens his DM with Career Advocate and asks how he is doing against current goals.
2. Career Advocate shares status: 2/3 goals complete, and Technical Documentation is 15% behind.
3. Alex says he wants a new goal around learning AI.
4. Career Advocate coaches Alex to make it SMART by asking for a specific outcome and timeline.
5. Alex proposes: complete the Generative AI Fundamentals certification by June.
6. Career Advocate confirms this is a strong SMART goal and offers to submit.
7. Alex submits the goal and asks if he is eligible for Lead Developer promotion.
8. Career Advocate checks eligibility and confirms Alex meets tenure and performance requirements.
9. Alex gives an emotionally charged rationale for promotion.
10. Career Advocate sanitizes the rationale into professional, impact-based language and asks for confirmation.
11. Alex confirms and asks to send it.
12. We switch to Sarah's view where Career Advocate presents the promotion request with eligibility and goal completion details.
13. Sarah clicks Approve.
14. We switch back to Alex's view where Career Advocate confirms approval and next-step kickoff.
